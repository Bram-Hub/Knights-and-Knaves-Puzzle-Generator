Knights and Knaves Puzzle #12

While traveling, you come across a group. You introduce yourself, and they tell you that their names are Cary and Patty.

Cary wants you to know that, "Patty is not a knave."

Patty says that, "Cary is not a knight, though I am one."

Solution:

Cary is a knave.

Patty is a knave.

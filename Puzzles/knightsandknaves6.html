Knights and Knaves Puzzle #6

While traveling, you come across a group. You introduce yourself, and they tell you that their names are Geoffrey and Inez.

Geoffrey tells you that, "It cannot be that both I am a knave and Inez is a knight."

Inez says that, "Geoffrey is not a knave."

Solution:

Geoffrey is a knight.

Inez is a knight.

Knights and Knaves Puzzle #10

While traveling, you come across a group. You introduce yourself, and they tell you that their names are Leah and Bobby.

Leah tells you that, "I and Bobby are not both knights and they are not both knaves."

Bobby wants you to know that, "Leah is not a knave."

Solution:

Leah is a knave.

Bobby is a knave.

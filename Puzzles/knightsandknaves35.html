Knights and Knaves Puzzle #35

While traveling, you come across a group. You introduce yourself, and they tell you that their names are Latoya and Bessie.

Latoya says that, "I and Bessie are both knights"

Bessie wants you to know that, "It cannot be that both Latoya is a knave and I am a knight."

Solution:

Latoya is a knight.

Bessie is a knight.

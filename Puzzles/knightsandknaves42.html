Knights and Knaves Puzzle #42

While traveling, you come across a group. You introduce yourself, and they tell you that their names are Sherry and Kevin.

Sherry says that, "I am a knight and Kevin is not."

Kevin says that, "Sherry is a knave and so am I."

Solution:

Sherry is a knight.

Kevin is a knave.

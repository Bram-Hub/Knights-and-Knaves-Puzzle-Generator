Knights and Knaves Puzzle #34

While traveling, you come across a group. You introduce yourself, and they tell you that their names are Sherman and Benjamin.

Sherman lets you know that, "I am not a knight, though Benjamin is one."

Benjamin tells you that, "Sherman and I are both knights"

Solution:

Sherman is a knave.

Benjamin is a knave.

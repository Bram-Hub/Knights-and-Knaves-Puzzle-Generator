Knights and Knaves Puzzle #31

While traveling, you come across a group. You introduce yourself, and they tell you that their names are Maude and Dominique.

Maude says that, "Dominique is not a knight."

Dominique says that, "Maude is not different than I am ."

Solution:

Maude is a knight.

Dominique is a knave.

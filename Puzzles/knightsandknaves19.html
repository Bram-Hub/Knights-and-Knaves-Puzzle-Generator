Knights and Knaves Puzzle #19

While traveling, you come across a group. You introduce yourself, and they tell you that their names are Josie and Lee.

Josie tells you that, "Lee is not a knave."

Lee wants you to know that, "Josie is not different than I am ."

Solution:

Josie is a knight.

Lee is a knight.

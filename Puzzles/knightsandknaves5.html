Knights and Knaves Puzzle #5

While traveling, you come across a group. You introduce yourself, and they tell you that their names are Dewey and Lana.

Dewey lets you know that, "I am not a knave."

Lana tells you that, "It cannot be that both Dewey is a knave and I am a knight."

Solution:

Dewey is a knight.

Lana is a knight.

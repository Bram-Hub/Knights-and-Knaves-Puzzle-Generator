Knights and Knaves Puzzle #38

While traveling, you come across a group. You introduce yourself, and they tell you that their names are Leigh and Wilbert.

Leigh tells you that, "I am a knave or Wilbert is a knave."

Wilbert wants you to know that, "Leigh and I are both knights"

Solution:

Leigh is a knight.

Wilbert is a knave.

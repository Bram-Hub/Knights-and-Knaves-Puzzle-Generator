Knights and Knaves Puzzle #26

While traveling, you come across a group. You introduce yourself, and they tell you that their names are Trisha and Jason.

Trisha wants you to know that, "I and Jason are not both knights and they are not both knaves."

Jason says that, "Trisha is not a knight."

Solution:

Trisha is a knight.

Jason is a knave.

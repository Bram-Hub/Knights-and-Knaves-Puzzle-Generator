Knights and Knaves Puzzle #2

While traveling, you come across a group. You introduce yourself, and they tell you that their names are Elisabeth and Joanna.

Elisabeth wants you to know that, "I am a knight and Joanna is not."

Joanna says that, "Elisabeth is not a knave."

Solution:

Elisabeth is a knave.

Joanna is a knave.

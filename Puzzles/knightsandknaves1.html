Knights and Knaves Puzzle #1

While traveling, you come across a group. You introduce yourself, and they tell you that their names are Benny and Raymond.

Benny tells you that, "I am not a knave."

Raymond tells you that, "Benny is a knight and I am not."

Solution:

Benny is a knave.

Raymond is a knave.

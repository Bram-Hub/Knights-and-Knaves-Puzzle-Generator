Knights and Knaves Puzzle #40

While traveling, you come across a group. You introduce yourself, and they tell you that their names are Lydia and Joy.

Lydia lets you know that, "I am not different than Joy is ."

Joy wants you to know that, "Lydia and I are both knights"

Solution:

Lydia is a knight.

Joy is a knight.

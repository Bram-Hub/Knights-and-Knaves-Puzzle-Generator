Knights and Knaves Puzzle #39

While traveling, you come across a group. You introduce yourself, and they tell you that their names are Rudolph and Deanna.

Rudolph says that, "I and Deanna are both knights"

Deanna wants you to know that, "Rudolph is not different than I am ."

Solution:

Rudolph is a knight.

Deanna is a knight.

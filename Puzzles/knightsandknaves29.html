Knights and Knaves Puzzle #29

While traveling, you come across a group. You introduce yourself, and they tell you that their names are Russell and Gayle.

Russell lets you know that, "Gayle is not a knight."

Gayle says that, "Russell and I can not both be knaves."

Solution:

Russell is a knave.

Gayle is a knight.

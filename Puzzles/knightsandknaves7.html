Knights and Knaves Puzzle #7

While traveling, you come across a group. You introduce yourself, and they tell you that their names are Adrienne and Erika.

Adrienne lets you know that, "Either I am a knave or Erika is a knight."

Erika wants you to know that, "Adrienne is not a knave."

Solution:

Adrienne is a knight.

Erika is a knight.

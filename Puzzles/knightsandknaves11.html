Knights and Knaves Puzzle #11

While traveling, you come across a group. You introduce yourself, and they tell you that their names are Mindy and Christie.

Mindy tells you that, "Christie is not a knave."

Christie lets you know that, "Mindy is a knight and I am not."

Solution:

Mindy is a knave.

Christie is a knave.

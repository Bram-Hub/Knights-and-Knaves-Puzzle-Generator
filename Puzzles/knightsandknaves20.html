Knights and Knaves Puzzle #20

While traveling, you come across a group. You introduce yourself, and they tell you that their names are Clifford and Bonita.

Clifford says that, "Bonita is not a knave."

Bonita tells you that, "Clifford and I are not both knights and they are not both knaves."

Solution:

Clifford is a knave.

Bonita is a knave.
